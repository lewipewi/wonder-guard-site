Early UTI Detection for Catheterized Patients

Wonder Guard is a smart biosensor system designed to detect UTIs early—improving patient safety and reducing hospital-acquired infections.

Get In Touch

Why Early Detection Matters

UTIs are one of the most common hospital-acquired infections—especially in long-term catheterized patients. By identifying early chemical signals of infection, Wonder Guard can reduce complications, shorten hospital stays, and improve outcomes.

Breakthrough Sensor Technology

Our nano-biosensor system is embedded into urinary catheter bags, detecting biomarkers in real time. The platform is designed for seamless hospital integration with data visualization and alerts.
